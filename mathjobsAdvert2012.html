Tenure-Track Faculty Positions
Department of Statistics
The Pennsylvania State University

The Department of Statistics at Penn State seeks to fill multiple tenure-track/tenured positions, at any level, to begin August 2013. Screening will begin November 15, 2012.

Basic Qualifications: Qualified applicants must demonstrate excellence in statistical research and teaching and have a Ph.D. in statistics or a related field completed or anticipated before August 2013.

Preferred Qualifications: All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply. Special consideration will be accorded to applicants who strengthen existing interdisciplinary ties of the Statistics Department, or who have the potential to establish new ties. Please see our more detailed job announcement for a description of existing interdisciplinary ties. Examples include, but are not limited to, experts in machine learning, astrostatistics, or spatial statistics, or those who could be hired in conjunction with a special interdisciplinary initiative of the Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences at Penn State . Also of interest are candidates who have professional actuarial credentials and a desire to work with Penn State's actuarial program.

More information about Penn State's department of statistics can be found in our more detailed job announcement or at www.stat.psu.edu.

Please direct any questions to facultysearch@stat.psu.edu.

The Pennsylvania State University is committed to affirmative action, equal opportunity, and the diversity of its workforce. Employment will require successful completion of background check(s) in accordance with University policies.
